Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the age calculation? expand_more

Our calculator uses precise algorithms accounting for leap years, time zones, and daylight saving changes. Results are accurate to the minute.

What date formats are supported? expand_more

We support all standard formats including MM/DD/YYYY, DD/MM/YYYY, and ISO 8601. You can also use our date picker for convenience.

Does it work across time zones? expand_more

Yes, our calculator automatically adjusts for time zones and daylight saving time differences between locations.

How are leap years handled? expand_more

We account for all leap years since 1900, including the special rules for century years (e.g., 2000 was a leap year but 1900 was not).

Can I calculate future ages? expand_more

Absolutely! Enter a future date in the "End Date" field to calculate how old someone will be at that future date.
